Turn an open-graph image into a thumbnail on Discord

There's only one thing which I love more than programming.
A well-formed embed; and naturally, I suck at it!

That's why, while building this website, I tried to implement a "thumbnail" on Discord, much like this:

The solution was fairly easy actually!
Even though it's not exactly documented, all you have to do is resize the image down using the og:image:{width,height} meta tags.

Here's an example:

<head>
    <meta property="og:image" content="/logo.png" />
    <meta property="og:image:type" content="image/png" />
    <meta property="og:image:width" content="250" />
    <meta property="og:image:height" content="250" />
</head>

Note that mixing Twitter and OpenGraph images may mess up your embed; but that's it, now you have your beautiful thumbnail to show off on Discord :)
